Aurora Manuel performs with the Maggio Musicale Fiorentino as assistant principal violist and violin. Raised in New York, she has studied under the guidance of Sally Thomas and Ivan Galamian, Lillian Fuchs and George Neikrug. Ms. Manuel also holds a degree from the Royal College of Music. She has participated in numerous festivals and has performed in chamber ensembles throughout the United States, Europe, and Asia.
