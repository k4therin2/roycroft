ELCOME to the Roycroft Chamber Music Festival Web site! The festival is held each year at St. Matthias Episcopal Church in East Aurora, New York, and is recognized for the excellence of its programming and performances. RCMF has become a highlight of Western New York's musical landscape and we are very excited about the prospects for the 2015 season. We hope you will join us this year.
